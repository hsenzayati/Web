Maldives

The Maldives is a small island nation in the Indian Ocean known for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and abundant marine life. Some popular activities in the Maldives include snorkeling, diving, and surfing.

The cuisine of the Maldives is largely influenced by Indian and Sri Lankan cuisine, with dishes like curries, fish soup, and samosas being popular choices.

Informations sur Maldives

Population: 530,000

Langue: Divehi
