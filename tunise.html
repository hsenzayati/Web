Tunisie

Tunisie is a North African country known for its beautiful beaches, ancient ruins, and diverse culture. Some popular attractions in Tunisia include the Bardo National Museum, Carthage, and the medina of Tunis.

Tunisia is also known for its delicious cuisine, which features a mix of Arabic, Mediterranean, and French influences. Some popular dishes include couscous, brik, and tajine.

Informations sur Tunisie

Population: 11.7 million

Langue: Arabe

Monnaie: Dinar tunisien (TND)
