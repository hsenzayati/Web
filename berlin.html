Berlin, Germany

Berlin is the capital and largest city of Germany. It is a city with a rich history, culture, and nightlife. Berlin is known for its landmarks such as the Brandenburg Gate, Berlin Wall, and Checkpoint Charlie.

Some other popular attractions in Berlin include the Berlin Zoo, Pergamon Museum, and the Berlin Cathedral.
