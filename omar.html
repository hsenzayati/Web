Omra, Saudi Arabia

Omra is a pilgrimage to the holy city of Mecca, Saudi Arabia. It is a spiritual journey for Muslims that can be taken at any time of the year, unlike the Hajj pilgrimage which has specific dates.

Some of the rituals involved in the Omra pilgrimage include performing Tawaf (circumambulation) around the Kaaba, Sa'i (running) between the hills of Safa and Marwah, and cutting or shaving of hair.
